Mission & vision

Opportunity for every individual.

Mission: Our mission is to provide equal opportunity for all individuals to learn and broaden their horizon through programs, educational classes, activities, and resources.

Vision: Our vision is that every individual be afforded opportunities that will improve their quality of life.

What we do

Programs that strengthen lives and communities.

Our work is rooted in education, positive activities, and practical resources, with youth as a primary focus and reentry support as a vital part of our mission.

Youth Development

Programs and guidance that help youth grow, learn, and make positive choices.

Reentry Support

Support for men and women returning home from state or federal facilities to help rebuild stable lives.

Community Activities

Events that create unity through positive interaction, service, and community care.

Educational Classes

Life-skills and awareness topics including financial literacy, health and wellness, and mentoring.

Resources

Help that meets real needs such as funding support, scholarships, and iPads for learning.

Local Outreach

Community-based service in Prentiss County and beyond, including support events at local jails.
