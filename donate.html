Support youth, reentry, and community unity.

From Prison to Purpose, Foundation is dedicated to the betterment of our communities in Prentiss County and beyond. Your gift helps us continue building programs that serve youth, strengthen families, and support men and women returning home.

What your gift supports

  • Youth development through mentoring, activities, and educational opportunities
  • Reentry support for men and women returning from state or federal facilities
  • Community activities such as events, sports, food drives, and back-to-school drives
  • Senior citizen care and community unity initiatives
  • Educational classes including financial literacy, health & wellness, mental health awareness, and law awareness
  • Resources like funding support, scholarships, and iPads for learning
